Welcome to BootDapp

What is this ?

  • This is a simple Dapp's helper.
  • It let you host your Dapps HTML HomePage on GitHub, and serve it as a bootdapp.com subdomain
  • For example, this page you are reading is here
  • To register a new dapp on bootdapp, you just have to fill a form inside this dapp, and fill 2 entry : the name of your dapp, and the github repository hosting your dapp's HTML
  • Bootdapp is also dapp
